I design and build production AI applications for businesses: document intelligence pipelines, AI voice agents, ETL and BI systems, and legal workflow automation. My approach is deterministic-first: code handles what code does best, LLMs handle what genuinely requires inference, and humans stay in the loop where accuracy and accountability matter.

Based in Colombia, working with clients across the US and LATAM.

## What I Build

- **Document intelligence**: PDF extraction pipelines combining deterministic parsing, OCR, and LLM Vision, with confidence scoring and human review gates
- **AI voice agents**: inbound call handling, intake classification, scheduling, and telephony integrations (Retell AI, Twilio, Google Calendar)
- **Data and BI pipelines**: API-to-warehouse ETL with orchestrated workflows feeding executive dashboards (PostgreSQL, Metabase, Snowflake)
- **Legal automation**: multi-phase document generation pipelines with structured extraction, validation gates, and CRM sync
- **Browser automation**: headless workflows for systems without APIs, including captcha resolution and IVR fallbacks

## How I Work

- Deterministic logic, heuristics, model inference, and human decisions are separated by design
- AI outputs are auditable: confirmed vs. inferred vs. ambiguous, never a black box
- Schemas, validations, and fallback paths before trusting any model output
- The simplest solution that solves the real problem without closing future paths
